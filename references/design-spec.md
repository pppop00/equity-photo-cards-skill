# Design Spec

This file is the strict publishing spec for all cards generated by this skill.

## Canvas

- Size: `1080 x 1350`
- Background: `#FCFCFD`
- Export target: direct social post image
- No drop shadows, no dark background, no fake paper textures
- Exception: the post-copy image may use a dark background inspired by social-post editors

## Typography

- Primary font: Arial family only
- Headings: bold Arial
- Body: regular Arial
- Body copy should use the enlarged mobile-readable baseline now used by the renderer and validator; increase readability first, but never at the cost of overflow or overlap
- Do not mix serif fonts
- Chinese and English should use the same family choice
- Letter spacing: `0`
- Do not add manual tracking or widened character spacing in titles
- Cover main title `每天学习一个公司`: 1 line only
- Company display name: use the short company name, for example `特斯拉`, not `特斯拉公司`

## Color System

- Primary text: `#111827`
- Secondary text: `#667085`
- Divider/stroke: `#EAECF0`
- Highlight panel: `#FFF7ED`
- Brand orange: `#B45309`
- Brand / emphasis red: `#E82127`
- Positive accent green: `#12B76A`
- Neutral accent gold: `#C9A35D`
- Data accent blue: `#1570EF`

## Global Layout

- Left top: `金融豹`
- Small brand subtitle below it: `FINANCE LEOPARD`
- Right top: page number only, for example `03`
- Horizontal divider below header
- Left bottom only: `公司名 | 日期`
- No right-top company monogram
- No footer brand copy
- Keep outer safe margin at `72px`
- Minimum vertical gap between major sections: `36px`
- Minimum gap between text blocks inside a section: `20px`
- Decorative circles must stay fully outside all text bounding boxes

## Copy Limits

- Cover title: 1 line
- Company name: 1 line
- Intro sentence: max 2 lines
- Metric cards: label 1 line, value 1 line
- Card 1 yellow panel paragraph: target `100` to `125` Chinese characters, max 7 lines
- Card 1 yellow panel paragraph should prefer 2 complete sentences over clipped thesis fragments
- Bullet sections: max 4 bullets
- Each bullet: max 3 to 4 lines depending on column width
- Numbers, percentages, tickers, and English words must stay on one line and may not be split across line breaks
- Approved English proper nouns such as `FSD`, `Robotaxi`, and `Optimus` may be kept in English
- Commas, periods, semicolons, colons, enumeration commas, and equivalent English punctuation may not start a new line
- A line beginning with `，。；：、,.!?` or equivalent closing punctuation is an automatic validation failure
- Card 2 industry paragraph: must be one complete summarized paragraph, target `140` to `180` Chinese characters, no truncation beyond its box
- Card 4 judgement paragraph: max `52` Chinese characters and must fit entirely inside the yellow box
- Card 5 yellow panel bullets: exactly 3 bullets, each max 28 Chinese characters
- Final brand card main statement: max 3 lines
- Card 6 title: max 2 lines
- Card 6 content: exactly 4 bullet lines, each max 2 lines
- Card 6 hashtags: max 4 lines and max 5 hashtags
- Any font increase must be checked against section bounds, spacing, and overlap before export
- Never allow overlap, clipping, or hidden text

## Card-by-Card Structure

## Card 1

- Title: `每天学习一个公司`
- Company name in red, use short company name only
- Small English/ticker line below company name
- Three metric cards in one row
- One rounded highlight panel titled `公司看点`
- The paragraph inside must be summarized from the HTML content into one complete paragraph
- The yellow panel should be visually filled to roughly 4 to 6 lines; large unused whitespace is a validation failure

## Card 2

- Left card: company background
- Company background should prefer 4 bullets when the source report has enough material
- Right card: Porter five forces bars
- Add one short industry paragraph
- The industry paragraph must be a complete summarized paragraph from the HTML Porter section, not clipped mid-sentence
- Add one short conclusion block
- The left card should feel dense and editorial, not like three isolated KPI notes floating in whitespace

## Card 3

- Revenue flow bars must stay the visual center
- Keep three margin metric cards below the bars
- Add exactly three explanation bullets
- This card is the most data-forward card
- Keep at least `36px` vertical separation between the chart block and the yellow explanation block
- Keep at least `20px` vertical separation between the last revenue row and the metric cards

## Card 4

- Two-column layout
- Left column: what the company makes money from now
- Right column: what to watch in the next 2 to 3 years
- Bottom right: one short judgement box
- This card must be especially checked for line overlap
- Keep at least `28px` internal padding in each text column
- Keep at least `20px` spacing between bullet groups and the judgement box
- Judgement box copy should prefer Chinese, but approved English proper nouns such as `FSD` and `Robotaxi` may remain in English if they fit cleanly
- In the two main columns, prefer using as much high-value copy as possible without overflow
- Both main columns should feel substantially filled; obvious vertical emptiness is a validation failure

## Card 5

- Publishable brand close only
- Large `金融豹`
- One short subheading such as `一句话看特斯拉`
- One strong red statement
- One rounded panel with 3 memory points
- Use `今天这家公司，特斯拉` style copy, not `今天这家公司：特斯拉公司`
- Yellow panel title must be `今日总结`
- One CTA line such as `关注金融豹，每天学习一个公司。`
- Do not include internal-branding meta copy or strategy notes
- Enlarge the yellow panel before allowing text truncation
- Remove the `今天这家公司，特斯拉` line entirely if it weakens the layout

## Card 6

- This is a dedicated post-copy image for Xiaohongshu and TikTok
- It must contain three sections only: `title`, `content`, and `hashtags`
- Title should be strong and direct, suitable for a social post cover line
- Content should be 4 concise bullet lines based on the company report
- Hashtags should be relevant to the company, sector, and account brand
- Hashtags must be curated, not sprayed: keep only the top 3 to 5 highest-signal tags
- The content must read like a real post draft, not internal notes

## Logo Rules

- Preferred: official company logo if available locally
- If no official logo is available, omit the logo
- Never synthesize a fake logo from ticker letters in the header
- If a logo is used, keep it inside the content area and avoid decorative dominance

## Prohibited Elements

- Dark backgrounds
- Decorative company monogram in the top right
- Mixed font families
- Overlapping text
- Tiny unreadable annotations
- Meta/internal copy about account strategy
- Extra footer text beyond `公司名 | 日期`
